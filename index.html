Meet Our Founder

Asim Hyderi

Founder & Principal

Mr. Asim Hyderi brings over 30 years of extensive experience in pharma engineering and validation to Prime Systems. With a background in Mechanical Engineering from N.E.D University and numerous specialized trainings, he has held key leadership positions in multinational pharmaceuticals for over a decade.

Professional Journey

Mr. Asim has served as Head of Engineering in multinational pharmaceuticals for over 10 years before establishing Prime Systems, where he has been the Principal for over 15 years. His expertise spans compliance with standards of WHO, MCC of South Africa, ISO, and corporate standards of multinational companies.

Expertise

Throughout his career, Mr. Asim has focused on pharma validation, developing and executing comprehensive validation protocols across all areas of pharmaceutical manufacturing.
